Plant Encyclopedia

Monstera Deliciosa

Known as the Swiss Cheese Plant, Monstera is popular for its large, perforated leaves and easy care.

Snake Plant

Also called Sansevieria, this resilient plant thrives in low light and purifies the air effectively.

Peace Lily

Famous for its white flowers, the Peace Lily is an easy indoor plant that filters toxins from the air.

Succulent

Succulents store water in their leaves, making them drought-resistant and perfect for beginners.

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Popular for its large violin-shaped leaves, this plant prefers bright, indirect light.

Aloe Vera

Known for its medicinal uses, Aloe Vera is a succulent that requires minimal watering.

Spider Plant

Spider Plants are easy to grow and great for removing pollutants from indoor air.

Boston Fern

Boston Ferns love humidity and indirect light, making them perfect for bathrooms or kitchens.

Rubber Plant

Known for its large glossy leaves, the Rubber Plant adds a tropical vibe to any room.

Jade Plant

A popular succulent with thick, shiny leaves; it symbolizes good luck and prosperity.

ZZ Plant

The ZZ Plant is nearly indestructible, tolerates low light and infrequent watering.

Lavender

Lavender is fragrant and calming, often used in aromatherapy and as an outdoor herb.
