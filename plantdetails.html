Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)

The Peace Lily is a graceful tropical plant known for its elegant white flowers and deep green leaves. It is a natural air purifier and thrives in indoor environments with low to medium light. Its peaceful appearance brings calm to any space, and with regular watering, it can bloom multiple times throughout the year.

Care Instructions

Light

Indirect sunlight

Water

Weekly

Soil

Well-draining

Temp

18–27°C

Humidity

Moderate–High

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is a popular succulent well-known for its medicinal and cosmetic uses. The gel inside its thick leaves soothes burns, moisturizes skin, and promotes healing. It thrives in warm, sunny spots and needs minimal watering, making it perfect for those new to gardening or looking for a low-maintenance plant with natural benefits.

Care Instructions

Light

Direct sunlight

Water

Every 2–3 weeks

Soil

Sandy, fast-draining

Temp

13–27°C

Humidity

Low

Snake Plant (Sansevieria)

The Snake Plant is a hardy, striking houseplant with tall, sword-shaped leaves. It's one of the best plants for filtering indoor air and even produces oxygen at night, making it ideal for bedrooms. It thrives in almost any light condition and can go weeks without water, making it perfect for beginners or low-maintenance spaces.

Care Instructions

Light

Low–Bright

Water

Every 2–4 weeks

Soil

Well-drained

Temp

15–30°C

Humidity

Low

Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

Spider Plants are easy to grow, fast to propagate, and perfect for hanging baskets. Their arching leaves and baby "spiderettes" add lively charm to any room. They're great air purifiers and thrive with minimal attention, making them a great starter plant or gift for new plant parents.

Care Instructions

Light

Bright, indirect

Water

1–2 times/week

Soil

General potting mix

Temp

18–24°C

Humidity

Moderate

Rubber Plant (Ficus elastica)

The Rubber Plant is admired for its broad, glossy leaves and upright growth. It can grow into a small indoor tree when given space and care. With regular watering and bright, filtered light, it develops strong stems and beautiful foliage. It’s a bold, dramatic plant that suits modern interiors and adds a tropical touch.

Care Instructions

Light

Bright, filtered

Water

Weekly

Soil

Well-draining

Temp

16–24°C

Humidity

Moderate

ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia)

The ZZ Plant is incredibly tough and stylish, with shiny, dark green leaves that tolerate neglect. It survives in low-light conditions, rarely needs watering, and resists pests and diseases. A great choice for offices or bedrooms, it adds a sophisticated look while requiring minimal care and attention.

Care Instructions

Light

Low–Medium

Water

Every 2–3 weeks

Soil

Well-drained

Temp

15–26°C

Humidity

Low–Moderate
